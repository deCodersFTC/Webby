Outreach

Outreach events

Our team organizes a range of outreach events. We participate in parades, code marathons, organizes summer camps, and come to schools to promote STEAM learning. One outreach effort we are currently working on is mentoring students in East Palo Alto,CA schools. We are working with an organization known as SEWA to help teach kids and inspire kids to pursue robotics in their future. So far we have been teaching at Costano Elementary School and we are working to broaden our the locations where we teach.
